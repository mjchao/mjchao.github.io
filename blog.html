Tips for Success in the CS Major

I graduated from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor in April, 2017 with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering. I did pretty well in my major. Looking back, there were a lot of hacks, techniques, and ideas that helped me succeed. Everyone's definition of success is different, but mine was getting good grades and internship experiences so that I had an excellent full-time job lined up upon graduating. I finished my degree in three years with a very high GPA and three internship experiences. In the posts here, I'll share some of these tips that might be helpful to you or at least provide an interesting perspective. I hope you enjoy the blog!
