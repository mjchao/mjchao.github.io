Tips for Success in the CS Major

I graduated from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor in April, 2017 with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering. I did pretty well in my major. Looking back, there were a lot of hacks, techniques, and ideas that helped me succeed. Everyone's definition of success is different, but mine was getting good grades and internships with the ultimate goal of finding an excellent full-time job after graduating. I finished my degree in three years with a very high GPA and three internship experiences. Now, I work full-time at a fantastic company that I don't want to ever leave. In the posts here, I'll share some tips that might be helpful to someone pursing a computer science major. I hope you enjoy the blog!
